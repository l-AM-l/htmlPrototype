Direct or immediate integrals

Calculus

Direct or immediate integrals are integrals that, due to their simplicity, do not require the application of an integration method for their resolution.

To solve these integrals we need to know the derivatives of the elementary functions (derivative table (PDF)), the chain rule and the properties of integrals (we will see them later).
